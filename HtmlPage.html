WELCOME to The MOMENT...

You've come a long way and your sobriety is one of the most important things in your life. We all know that life gets hectic. When chaos abounds, keep calm with "In the moment..."

  • Create a unique and personalized recovery and relapse prevention plan.
  • Identify times and triggers and travel destinations that tempt you to use. Get alerts reminding you of your successes at times of day where keeping calm is the most difficult.
  • Record your sobriety succeses.
  • Keep a record of meetings you attended as well as on going meetings in your area.
  • Keep a daily log of your moods and temptations (you could chose to send this daily data to your sponsor and/ or therapist as well).
  • Stay in touch with sponsors and supporters.
  • Learn to be mindful in the moment instead of being impulsive.
  • Learn new habits and identify new helps that will encourage you on your journey through sobriety.
